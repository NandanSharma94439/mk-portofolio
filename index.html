The Journey Behind MK Trading Academy

My trading journey began with curiosity about how financial markets move. After exploring different strategies and indicators, I realized the most valuable information was already on the chart itself. That discovery led me to dedicate myself to Price Action and Market Structure.

Over four years of studying and practicing Price Action, trading became more than a profession—it became a discipline. My goal is to help students think independently, understand the logic behind every trade, and build confidence through knowledge instead of shortcuts.

Trading Principles

Trading Principles

My approach to trading is built on discipline, patience, and consistency rather than chasing quick profits. Every trade follows a structured plan with calculated risk, clear reasoning, and complete emotional control.

01

Discipline

Every trade follows a predefined plan without emotional decisions. Consistency comes from respecting the process, not predicting the market.

02

Risk Management

Capital preservation comes first. Every position has a predefined risk, ensuring that no single trade can significantly impact long-term growth.

03

Patience

The market rewards those who wait for high-probability opportunities instead of forcing unnecessary trades.

04

Consistency

Sustainable success is achieved by repeating good habits every day, regardless of short-term wins or losses.
